An introduction is in order…

Welcome to our place on the web – we're glad you're here. The name of this place is Pikoru, and through this gateway we receive those who are interested in us and what we do.

Who are we?

A creative trio effectively completing each other with our diverse range of skillsets, experiences and beard lengths.

What do we do?

In short, we are creators. We make games and other interactive pieces that grow from our unifying enthusiasm for great innovation and style. We also create our own game media content.
